Pier Woudstra is an instrument builder & performing artist based in Utrecht, currently studying Music & Technology at the University of the Arts Utrecht.
The common thread in his work is the blending of traditional (folk) musical ideas with contemporary (often electronic) techniques.
Lately this interest has expanded to permacomputing, feedback loops & complex systems.
