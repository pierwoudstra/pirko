ram6 is an ode to the singer Ramses Shaffy made possible with electronics, perfomed live with Laura Kampman, Bram van Klink & Milo Mooi Wilten.

the project was carried out using a no input mixer, samples played using multiple iPhones and manipulating of a sample via Ableton controlled with a customized dj controller.
